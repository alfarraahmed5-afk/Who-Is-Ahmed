VMT Regression Analysis — NHTS

OLS regression on 12,507 NHTS households identifying determinants of household vehicle miles travelled.

  • Final model R²=0.20, F(8,328)=10.25, p<.001

  • Policy implications: vehicle ownership reduction & cycling investment are highest-leverage interventions
